Haritdrishti Vimana

Drone with Green Vision

Haritdrishti Vimana is an AI-based hybrid waste management system that integrates aerial drones and autonomous land rovers to identify, collect, and segregate waste, with a special focus on reducing plastic pollution in well-maintained public and private spaces.

Objective

  • Develop a hybrid waste management system using drones and autonomous rovers
  • Improve waste identification and collection efficiency in tourist spots, landmarks, green zones, smart cities and private estates
  • Use AI-based image classification to segregate biodegradable, non-biodegradable and plastic waste
  • Reduce human labour and exposure to unhygienic waste
  • Encourage eco-friendly alternatives such as compostable bags, paper and bamboo products

Background / Problem Statement

Waste management remains a major environmental challenge, especially in areas with high visitor movement and large infrastructure. Traditional manual methods are labour-intensive, time-consuming and often result in poor segregation.

Haritdrishti Vimana introduces a futuristic solution by combining aerial monitoring with AI-driven autonomous ground collection, making waste management more organized, scalable and efficient, particularly in well-maintained environments.

Working Principle

  1. A drone (SkyEye) surveys the area and identifies waste hotspots
  2. GPS coordinates of detected waste locations are generated
  3. The autonomous rover receives the coordinates
  4. Raspberry Pi processes images to classify waste using AI
  5. Arduino-controlled mechanisms segregate waste into appropriate bins
  6. The drone continues aerial monitoring for verification

Methodology

  • Drone-based area scanning and data collection
  • Wireless transmission of GPS and image data
  • Autonomous navigation with obstacle detection using ultrasonic sensors
  • AI-based waste identification at each hotspot
  • Separate tracking of plastic waste for sustainability analysis
  • Continuous repetition until all waste hotspots are cleared

Components Used

Hardware
  • Drone with GPS & Camera
  • 4WD Robotic Rover
  • Raspberry Pi
  • Arduino Uno
  • Ultrasonic Sensors
  • Servo / Flip Motors
  • Battery (Optional Solar Panel)
Software
  • Python (TensorFlow / OpenCV)
  • Arduino IDE
  • GPS Mapping Software
  • Data Logging & Analysis Tools

Applications

Golf Courses
Historic Monuments & Tourist Places
Smart Cities & Developed Roads
Religious & Cultural Sites
Large Farmhouses & Private Estates
Monitoring of Normal Roads

Advantages

  • Reduces dependency on manual labour
  • Accurate AI-based waste segregation
  • Efficient coverage of large and complex areas
  • Promotes eco-friendly alternatives by tracking plastic waste
  • Provides aerial monitoring and reporting
  • Scalable for multi-drone and multi-rover deployment

Future Scope

  • Integration of multiple autonomous drones
  • Expansion to classify glass, metal and textile waste
  • Smart city integration for centralized monitoring
  • Data-driven insights for sustainable planning

Conclusion

Haritdrishti Vimana presents a sustainable and innovative approach to modern waste management by integrating AI, drones and autonomous rovers. The system minimizes human effort while ensuring efficient waste identification, segregation and monitoring, making it highly suitable for organized, high-profile environments.
